What is Similarity?

"Our minds group things with similar properties such as color or shape. 'Like goes with like.' In layout we can use similarity to create order and organization through unity."(pg 62)

"The Gestalt law of similarity predicts that people will group items with similar properties such as shape, size, or color."(pg 280)

White Space is Not Your Enemy by Rebecca Hagen and Kim Golombisky. Published in 2017 by Taylor and Francis Group, LLC.

Personal Definition

Similarity in a design allows readers to group objects of similar features together. The viewers attention will be caught through similar colors, shapes, and sizes. If the design shows similarity it often shows a sense of unity or togetherness.
