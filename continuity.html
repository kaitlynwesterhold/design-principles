What is Continuity?

"The Gestalt law of continuity predicts the way the eye will follow the direction of a line."(pg 273)

"Our eyes follow the lines tying screen elements together both vertically and horizontally. Intentional alignment of layout elements, whether it's the top edges of copy blocks, creates these lines."(pg 63)

White Space is Not Your Enemy by Rebecca Hagen and Kim Golombisky. Published in 2017 by Taylor and Francis Group, LLC.

Personal Definition

The eyes of the reader will continue to follow patterns even after the pattern has ended. The lines direct the readers eyes to continue on in a direction or pattern. Continuity helps the readers eyes to flow through out the elements.
