What is Unity?

"Unity means that all parts of the design work together, and everything looks like it belongs together."(pg 60)

"A layout is visually unified if its different parts have visual links or relationships to one another. A good design has some consistency in terms of the pattern of type columns, or rhythm of typography, or style of visuals, etc. Unity refers to oneness, that the result is one cohesive design or visual message."(pg 60)

White Space is Not Your Enemy by Rebecca Hagen and Kim Golombisky. Published in 2017 by Taylor and Francis Group, LLC.

Personal Definition

Unity refers to the cohesiveness of a design. All elements of your design should come together to form one unified design. You can create a sense of unity through movements, flow or rhythm. Even if each element of design is different; the design should still all flow together and create a sense of unity.
